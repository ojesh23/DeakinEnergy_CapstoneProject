About Energy Safe Victoria

Energy Safe Victoria is the regulator responsible for electrical and gas safety in the state. This statutory authority audits the design, construction and maintenance of electricity and gas networks and installations and ensures that appliances meet stringent safety and energy efficiency standards before they are sold. Energy Safe Victoria also conducts comprehensive public awareness safety campaigns to educate the community on the potential dangers of gas and electricity.



What is this project all about?

Electricity distribution businesses reports any electrical network-related incidents to Energy Safe Victoria as part of their regulatory obligations. This project aims to analyse these datasets to see any trends and patterns that lead to these incidents from occurring.



Our Aims

  1. Determine the most influential factors related to network-related incidents
  2. Analyse the degree of influence each factors may have to predict an incident from occurring
  3. Identify patterns of different types of incidents depending on environmental, technical, community, and work factors, among others.
  4. Create a predictive model that could classify the incidents based on the data.
